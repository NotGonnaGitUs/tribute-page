Most of us if given the option choose the path of least resistance. We take stable career paths and lead a peaceful life. Elon, has spent his life pursuing the most difficult of challenges facing our species today.

Here are some of his current goals:

  • Reducing global warming through sustainable energy production and consumption
  • Setting up a human colony on Mars thereby reducing the risk of human extinction by making life multiplanetary
  • Creating an Artificial Intelligence which is beneficial to humanity

A timeline of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Born in South Africa.
  • 1983 - Creates a video game called Blastar and sells it for an equivalent of $500 at the age of 12.
  • 1988 - Graduates from Pretoria Boys High School distinctions in science and computer studies.
  • 1991 - After attending Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, transfers to the University of Pennsylvania and completes a BS in Economics (Wharton) and a BA with a major in physics.
  • 1995 - To join the Internet boom moves to Silicon Valley deferring his graduate program in applied physics and materials science.
  • 1999 - Sells a company created with his brother called Zip2 to Compaq making $22 million for himself. Creates X.com which is the domain for PayPal.
  • 2002 - Becomes a citizen of America and founds SpaceX.
  • 2004 - Becomes an investor in Tesla Motors,
  • 2007 - SpaceX acquires a $1.6 Billion contract to bring cargo to International Space Station.
  • 2008 - Becomes Tesla's CEO,
  • 2010 - Tesla launches an Initial Public Offer.
  • 2012 - SpaceX successfully delivers a payload to International Space Station and Tesla delivers all-electric Model S.
  • 2013 - releases the concept of Hyperloop.
  • 2015 - creation of OpenAI with a goal to develop artificial intelligence in a way that is safe and beneficial to humanity.

How

Musk attributes most of his success to sheer hard work. And if you read more about his life through the links below, it is hard to argue with that.

The fact that he has taken on the toughest challenges shows the determination he possesses and the trail of success he has left behind proves his intellect in meeting those challenges.

Here is a man who is working towards the biggest challenges we face. This forces us to ask ourselves a question which we evade for far too long
what are we doing with our time today?
